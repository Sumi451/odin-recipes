Description

Masala chai is a popular Indian tea made by brewing black tea with milk and water, and sweetening it with sugar. The name translates to "mixed-spice tea".

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Tea Leaves
  • Sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • Bay leaf

Steps

  1. Pour milk in a sauce pan.
  2. Add tea leaves , Bay leaf, small amount of Black Pepper, 2 teaspoons of sugar(for one person)
  3. Let it boil until you can smell the fragrance of the tea or till you can see the colour turn to light brown.
  4. Serve it hot in a terracotta pot(preferred)
