Steam Rice Recipe

Home

Description

Steamed rice is a simple preparation of rice where the grains are cooked primarily through steam, resulting in a fluffy, separate texture, often served plain and commonly used as a base for various dishes, with the cooking method involving a combination of boiling and steaming the rice until tender

Ingredients

  • Any type of rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Take rice as per your need and soak wash or rinse it 3 to 4 times.
  2. Put the rice inside a pressure cooker and pour water inside the cooker in a 1:2 ratio for rice and water respectively.
